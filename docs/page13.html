Join us and make a real difference saving lives

The Center for Breakthrough Medicines (CBM) has a remarkable team of people that includes some of the brightest minds in the industry. We are building the extraordinary and we invite you to join us in our mission.

Be a part of the team developing next generation medicines

Everyone at the Center for Breakthrough Medicines (CBM) is passionate about saving and improving patients’ lives. This drives us every day and we are always seeking to add like-minded talent to our team.

We have one of the most experienced teams in the industry – pioneers in cell & gene therapy – and the largest single site facility in the world that is truly state-of-the-art and located in an ecosystem of heavily invested, world-class companies.

A culture of innovation, focused on our patient-centric vision

When you join CBM, you are immersed in a culture dedicated to progressing advanced therapies as fast as possible. We empower our people to share ideas, innovate and collaborate, as every voice is valued in the mission of bringing life-changing therapies to patients in need. You will be among a team of peers and mentors with strong scientific and technical prowess, and proven experience in bringing therapies to market. They will inspire you to play your part in the next breakthrough. With cutting-edge technology supporting you, industry pioneers surrounding you, and a patient-centric company behind you, there is no limit to what you can achieve.
