Relentlessly Patient-Focused

A commitment to patient’s lives, exceeding client expectations, and fostering exceptional customer relationships is at the core of what we do at CBM.

CBM is on a mission to save lives. It guides everything we do.

CBM is an innovative platform company solving critical manufacturing challenges and bringing Advanced Therapies to market faster with exceptional quality. Our mission is to dramatically shorten development timelines, greatly reduce regulatory risks and accelerate cures for patients.

At CBM there is one thing we all have in common - a passion to lead with science, and develop platforms and processes that deliver these cures to our clients faster, giving us a chance to impact patients’ lives.

Success means more than scale- our remarkable operational structure reflects our ability to provide exceptional service, quality and speed

“We have put together the right set of skill capabilities, mindset and culture in order to circumvent and master all of the challenges together, as a true partner in the development process.”

Joerg Ahlgrimm, President & CEO
