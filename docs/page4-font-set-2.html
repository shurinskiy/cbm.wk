Find your place and make a breakthrough.

CBM exists to bring medical breakthroughs to patients in need. Our mission is to save lives by accelerating the development and manufacturing of advanced therapies.

Join a team focused on saving patient lives and be part of the breakthrough at CBM.

At CBM, you’ll work alongside leading academic institutions and world-class technical partners on our state-of-the-art campus in a fast-paced, intellectual environment that empowers you to grow, experiment, and challenge the status quo.

Our culture is collaborative, entrepreneurial, and patient-centric. We provide our teams the space, mentorship, and opportunity to develop and manufacture life-saving therapeutics, getting them into the market, and into patients’ hands, quicker.

Patient-Centric Vision Drives the Mission

CBM strives for a future in which cell and gene therapy will be available and affordable to every patient that needs them. To make this vision a reality, we share a passion to lead with science and develop platforms and processes that deliver these cures to our clients faster. We have the unparalleled expertise, mindset and culture to master the challenges together as a true partner in a patient-focused approach to Advanced Therapy development.
